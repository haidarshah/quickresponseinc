FOOT SWITCH FOR POWER TOOL 120V

The Foot Switch is an extremely handy accessory designed for use with scroll saws, table saws, routers, and drill presses. It provides a convenient way to control power tools, ensuring that they only operate when the switch is engaged. This switch enhances safety and efficiency in your workshop by allowing hands-free operation.

Features

  • Hands-Free Operation: Activates power tools only when stepped
    on, ensuring they turn off when you step off.
  • Wide Compatibility: Suitable for use with scroll saws, table saws, routers, and drill presses.
  • Enhanced Safety: Prevents accidental start-up of power tools, improving overall safety in the workspace.
  • Durable Construction: Made in an ISO 9001 factory, ensuring high quality and reliability.
  • High Load Capacity: Supports a maximum load of 115 volts and 12 amps, making it suitable for various power tools.

Specifications

  • Voltage: 115 volts
  • Current Capacity: 12 amps
  • Operation Mode: Foot-activated (ON when stepped on, OFF when stepped off)
  • Manufacturing Standard: ISO 9001
