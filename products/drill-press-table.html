DRILL PRESS TABLE B2660

This Universal Drill Press Table is designed to fit most drill press models. The large table has a quick clamping system that will not damage your original tabletop and adds versatility to your press by giving you a larger workspace and increasing accuracy. To complete this package, the universal tabletop comes complete with a 3" fence and a stop block which both slide along the T-track slot.

Features

  • Quick clamping system: Ensures easy attachment without damaging the original tabletop.
  • Versatile workspace: Increases accuracy and provides a larger work area.
  • 3" fence and stop block: Both components slide along the T-track slot for precise adjustments.

Specifications

  • Table dimensions: 11 13/16" x 24" x 7/8"
  • Fence dimensions: 3" x 23 5/8" x 7/8"
  • Stop block dimensions: 2 1/2" x 2 7/8"

Comes With

  • Universal drill press table
  • 3" fence
  • Stop block
