ARBOR MT2/J6

The Arbor MT2 J6 is a high-precision tool designed to ensure secure mounting of drill chucks with a J6 taper onto machines with an MT2 spindle. Engineered for accuracy and durability, this arbor is a reliable choice for professional drilling and machining tasks. Its precise taper alignment minimizes runout and ensures consistent performance, making it ideal for metalworking, woodworking, and other demanding applications.

Features

  • Securely mounts drill chucks featuring a J6 taper to MT2 spindles.
  • Ensures precise alignment for accurate and reliable drilling operations.
  • Designed to minimize runout for smooth and consistent machining performance.
  • Sturdy construction for long-lasting durability and heavy-duty use.
  • Ideal for various machining applications requiring precision and stability.

Specifications

  • Taper: MT2 (spindle) to J6 (drill chuck).
  • Material: High-grade steel for enhanced durability and wear resistance.
  • Compatibility: Fits machines with an MT2 spindle and J6 taper drill chucks.
  • Design: Precision-machined for secure, wobble-free operation.

Comes With

  • One Arbor MT2 J6 (ready to use).
