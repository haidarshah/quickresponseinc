ARBOR MT2/J6

The Arbor MT2 J6 is a high-precision tool designed to ensure secure mounting of drill chucks with a J6 taper onto machines with an MT2 spindle. Engineered for accuracy and durability, this arbor is a reliable choice for professional drilling and machining tasks. Its precise taper alignment minimizes runout and ensures consistent performance, making it ideal for metalworking, woodworking, and other demanding applications.
