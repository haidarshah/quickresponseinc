This 13” Bench Top Planer with Helical Style Cutterhead features a powerful 2HP, 120V motor and 6 row helical cutterhead with 26 indexable cutters giving you power and superior finish you would expect from a stationary machine. This machine is slightly larger than our popular CX125HC 12.5” planer and it also has some added features. These additional features include 2 speeds, top mounted feed rollers, larger infeed & outfeed tables and pre-set depth stops for repeat cuts at 1/8", 1/4", 1/2", 3/4", 1", 1-1/4", 1-1/2" and 1-3/4".

Designed to manage larger, deeper cuts in wood, the helical style cutterhead is quieter and delivers a smoother finish compared to straight knife planers which will reduce your sanding time. Complete with built in dust collection with dual ports at 2-1/2" & 4" for easy hook up to a shop vac or dust collector.

The cutterhead raises and lowers on a 4-column precision ground carriage system which drastically reduces snipe. The convenient side carry handles allow for ease of portability and blade changes on this machine are simple and super fast. Chip an insert cutter? Simply unscrew the indexable cutter and rotate to get a fresh new cutting edge. Say goodbye to cumbersome and time-consuming blade changes with traditional straight knife planers.
