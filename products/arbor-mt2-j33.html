The Arbor MT2 J33 is a high-precision tool designed for securely mounting drill chucks with a J33 taper onto machines equipped with an MT2 spindle. Built to withstand rigorous use, it provides smooth operation with minimal runout, delivering reliable performance in a variety of metalworking applications.
