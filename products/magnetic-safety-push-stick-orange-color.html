MAGNETIC SAFETY PUSH STICK

The B3484 Magnetic Safety Push Stick is designed to keep your hands safely away from table saw and jointer blades while providing secure control over your workpiece. Featuring two built-in magnets, it easily attaches to cast iron surfaces for convenient storage when not in use. The V-groove and notched end help hold the workpiece down firmly, ensuring greater accuracy and safety during operation.

Features

  • Enhances safety by keeping hands away from saw and jointer blades
  • Two built-in magnets for easy attachment to cast iron surfaces
  • V-groove and notched end provide a secure grip on the workpiece
  • Ideal for use with table saws and jointers
  • Ergonomic handle design for comfortable and controlled use

Specifications

  • Model: B3484
  • Tool Type: Magnetic Safety Push Stick
  • Magnet Count: 2
  • Application: Table saws, jointers, and other woodworking machinery
