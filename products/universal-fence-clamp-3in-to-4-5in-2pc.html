UNIVERSAL FENCE CLAMP 3" TO 4.5" 2PC

The B3059 2-pc Universal Fence Clamps make securing shop-made auxiliary fences quick and effortless. Designed to hold fences from 3" to 4-1/2" thick, these clamps attach without interfering with your workpiece, ensuring smooth and accurate cuts.

To use, simply drill a 7/16" hole into the top of your auxiliary fence, insert the 3/8" fence clamp, and tighten the lock knobs for a firm and secure fit. These clamps help protect your original fence while allowing for easy customization of jigs and work setups.

Features:

  • Holds auxiliary fences 3" to 4-1/2" thick securely in place.
  • No interference with workpieces – Clamps attach to the top of the fence.
  • Protects your original fence from damage.
  • Easy installation – Requires a 7/16" hole in the auxiliary fence.
  • Durable construction – Built for long-term shop use.

Specifications:

  • Clamp Size: 3/8"
  • Required Hole Size: 7/16"
  • Fence Thickness Range: 3" to 4-1/2"
  • Quantity: 2 clamps per set

Comes With:

  • Two universal fence clamps

A must-have for woodworkers and DIYers looking to enhance their fence system without damaging their original setup!
