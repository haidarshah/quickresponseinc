32" ALUMINUM MITER T TRACK

This Aluminum Miter Track is a versatile and essential addition to any woodworking setup. This 32-inch track is designed to work seamlessly with standard 3/4" x 3/8" miter gauges, whether they come with or without a T-slot. It effortlessly slides into miter tracks or miter slots on your table saw, making it a perfect fit for table saw extensions, drill press tables, and band saw tables. The track features five pre-drilled holes, ensuring easy and secure installation.
