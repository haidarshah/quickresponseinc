32" ALUMINUM MITER T TRACK

This Aluminum Miter Track is a versatile and essential addition to any woodworking setup. This 32-inch track is designed to work seamlessly with standard 3/4" x 3/8" miter gauges, whether they come with or without a T-slot. It effortlessly slides into miter tracks or miter slots on your table saw, making it a perfect fit for table saw extensions, drill press tables, and band saw tables. The track features five pre-drilled holes, ensuring easy and secure installation.

Features

  • Compatible with standard 3/4" x 3/8" miter gauges, with or without T-slot
  • Slides into miter track or miter slot on your table saw
  • Suitable for use on table saw extensions, drill press tables, and band saw tables
  • Features 5 pre-drilled holes for easy installation
  • Durable aluminum construction for long-lasting performance

Specifications

  • Length: 32 inches
  • Material: Aluminum
  • Compatibility: Standard 3/4" x 3/8" miter gauges
  • Number of Pre-Drilled Holes: 5
  • Applications: Table saws, drill press tables, band saw tables
