VISE WOODWORKING 16" QUICK RELEASE

The 16" Woodworking Vise with Quick Release is a traditional pattern woodworking vise equipped with a quick-release mechanism. Simply pressing the trigger initiates quick action, allowing the front jaw to slide freely, enabling smooth interchange of large or small workpieces. Releasing the trigger returns the mechanism to normal for fine adjustments, making it highly useful for repetitive work situations.

Equipped with an adjustable front dog, this vise can be used with a bench stop to securely hold oversized workpieces that are too large to be clamped between the jaws. This dual functionality provides both a normal vise and a tail vise in one tool.

Manufactured from close-grained grey cast iron, this robust vise features accurately machined housings for smooth sliding movement and true parallel action, free of side or cross movement.

Features

  • Quick-release mechanism for easy adjustment and quick changes
  • Adjustable front dog for holding oversized workpieces
  • Manufactured from close-grained grey cast iron for durability
  • Accurately machined housings for smooth and precise operation
  • "TOE-IN" feature for even clamping along the whole depth of the workpiece
  • Buttress-threaded steel main screw for smooth and responsive adjustment
  • Provision for attaching wooden liners to protect the vise, workpiece, and tools

Specifications

  • Jaw Width: 10 1/2"
  • Jaw Opening: 16"
  • Net Weight: 17 kg

Comes With

  • Adjustable front dog
  • Provision for attaching wooden liners (liners not included)
