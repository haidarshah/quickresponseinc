VISE WOODWORKING 16" QUICK RELEASE

The 16" Woodworking Vise with Quick Release is a traditional pattern woodworking vise equipped with a quick-release mechanism. Simply pressing the trigger initiates quick action, allowing the front jaw to slide freely, enabling smooth interchange of large or small workpieces. Releasing the trigger returns the mechanism to normal for fine adjustments, making it highly useful for repetitive work situations.
