BENCH SCREW FOR WORK BENCH VISE

The 16" Bench Screw is a robust component designed to help you build your own custom vise. It features a 1 1/8" Acme threaded steel shaft, providing 13" of smooth travel for efficient clamping operations. The rugged cast iron fittings come pre-drilled with holes for secure attachment to both the workbench and the movable jaw of the vise. Additionally, a 1" diameter hole is included to accommodate the handle, making it easy to operate.

Features

  • Heavy-Duty Construction: 1 1/8" Acme threaded steel shaft ensures durability and precision.
  • Ample Travel: Provides 13" of travel for wide clamping capabilities.
  • Cast Iron Fittings: Rugged cast iron with pre-drilled holes for easy attachment to your workbench and vise jaw.
  • Custom Vise: Ideal for building your own vise, offering flexibility in design and setup.
  • Handle Hole: Includes a 1" diameter hole for inserting a handle, allowing for smooth operation.

Specifications

  • Shaft Size: 1 1/8" Acme threaded steel
  • Travel Length: 13"
  • Material: Steel shaft, cast iron fittings
  • Handle Hole Diameter: 1"

Comes With

  • 1 x 1 1/8" Acme threaded steel shaft
  • Cast iron fittings with pre-drilled holes for mounting
