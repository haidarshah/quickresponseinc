EXTRA LONG SAFETY PUSH PAD

The B3104 Extra Long Safety Push Pad is designed to provide enhanced control and safety when working with table saws, jointers, routers, and other woodworking machinery. Made from strong ABS plastic, this 16" long and 2-3/4" wide push pad offers a comfortable grip while keeping hands safely away from cutting blades.

Equipped with a sticky pad surface, it ensures a secure hold on workpieces, reducing the risk of slipping and improving precision. An essential safety tool for any woodworker or DIY enthusiast.

Features:

  • Extra-long design – 16" length provides greater reach and control.
  • Strong ABS plastic construction – Durable and lightweight for long-term use.
  • Sticky pad surface – Ensures a strong grip on materials for added safety.
  • Ergonomic grip – Comfortable to hold, reducing hand fatigue.
  • Enhances safety – Keeps hands safely away from blades and cutters.

Specifications:

  • Material: ABS plastic
  • Length: 16"
  • Width: 2-3/4"

Comes With:

  • One extra-long safety push pad
