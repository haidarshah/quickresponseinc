10 PCS BRASS ROUTER BUSHING SETS

Expand the capability of your router with the B3939 10 Piece Brass Router Bushing Set. This set transforms your router into a precision cutting machine, ideal for duplicating patterns, cutting signs, creating dovetails, or any other project requiring template work. Unlike bearing-tipped bits which can fail, this heavy-duty, precision-machined brass bushing set ensures worry-free routing. All guides protrude 0.240" for use with any template material of 1/4" thickness and larger. These guides are compatible with any router sub-base, offering versatility and ease of use.

Features

  • Precision Machined Brass: Ensures durable and accurate performance.
  • Wide Compatibility: Fits any router sub-base for maximum versatility.
  • Template Ready: Guides protrude 0.240" to work with template materials of 1/4" thickness and larger.
  • Versatile Applications: Ideal for pattern duplication, sign cutting, dovetail creation, and other template-based projects.
  • Heavy Duty: Provides reliable performance, unlike bearing-tipped bits which can fail.

Specifications

  • Set Includes: 10 pieces of brass bushings
