SHOP FOX MOBILE BASE 1000LB CAP D2057A

D2057A Adjustable Mobile Base with 1000 lbs Capacity- ShopFox Adjustable from 20" x 20" to 29-1/2" x 29-1/2" New & improved version of the popular Shop Fox mobile bases features better overall construction & design. Used for a stable and mobile platform upon which to mount machinery and equipment having a variety of base sizes and weights. New and improved castors are arranged on outriggers allowing the machine to sit as low as possible and yet be extremely stable and have excellent maneuverability. The base can be assembled in any of eight configurations providing maximum flexibility. The two-piece retractable feet uses earth magnets to allow the knob to turn while the foot pad is tight to the floor. Strong, extremely stable, outrigger supports. Capacity: 1000 lbs.
