BLADE GUIDE BEARING 2PC FOR 15" 16" BAND

The "Ball Bearing" Band Saw Blade Guides are designed for use with 14 1/2" band saws. This 2-piece set provides support both above and below the table, ensuring optimal performance and precision during cutting operations. The guides feature cast iron brackets that support 1 1/8" sealed ball bearings and are cam adjusted for exact fitting. These guides fit 7/8" diameter upper blade supports and are designed for long life adjustment, contributing to a longer blade life.

Features
• Designed for 14 1/2" band saws
• 2-piece set for above and below the table
• Cast iron brackets for sturdy support
• 1 1/8" sealed ball bearings
• Cam adjusted for precise fitting
• Fits 7/8" diameter upper blade supports
• Ensures long life adjustment and extended blade life

Specifications
• Type: Band Saw Blade Guides
• Compatibility: 14 1/2" Band Saw
• Bearing Size: 1 1/8"
• Upper Blade Support Diameter: 7/8"
• Material: Cast Iron Brackets, Sealed Ball Bearings

Comes With
• 2 Ball Bearing Band Saw Blade Guides (one for above and one for below the table)
