BLADE GUIDE BEARING 2PC FOR 15" 16" BAND

The "Ball Bearing" Band Saw Blade Guides are designed for use with 14 1/2" band saws. This 2-piece set provides support both above and below the table, ensuring optimal performance and precision during cutting operations. The guides feature cast iron brackets that support 1 1/8" sealed ball bearings and are cam adjusted for exact fitting. These guides fit 7/8" diameter upper blade supports and are designed for long life adjustment, contributing to a longer blade life.
