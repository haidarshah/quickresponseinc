ARBOR MT3/JT3

The B113 Drill Chuck Arbor is a high-precision tool designed for securely mounting drill chucks onto machine spindles. Made from precision-hardened and ground tool steel, it ensures durability, accuracy, and long-lasting performance. This arbor features an MT3 (Morse Taper 3) to JT3 (Jacobs Taper 3) configuration, making it ideal for drill presses, lathes, and milling machines requiring a reliable chuck mounting solution.

Features:

  • Made from precision-hardened and ground tool steel for superior strength
  • Provides a secure and accurate connection between the spindle and chuck
  • Designed for heavy-duty machining applications
  • Ensures minimal runout for improved drilling accuracy
  • Compatible with drill presses, lathes, and milling machines

Specifications:

  • Taper Type: MT3 (Morse Taper 3) to JT3 (Jacobs Taper 3)
  • Material: Precision-hardened and ground tool steel
  • Application: Used for mounting drill chucks onto machine spindles

Comes With:

  • 1 x Drill Chuck Arbor (MT3-JT3)
