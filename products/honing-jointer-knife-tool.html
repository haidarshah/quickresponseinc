HONING JOINTER KNIFE TOOL

Jointer Knives Honing Tool for H.S.S Knives

This simple hone tool lets you hone your jointer knives without having to

against the cutter head for perfect edges. The body holds the hones (180-400 grit
aluminum oxide stone). Indexed to the cutter head for perfect edges.
A great tool for all jointer knives.
