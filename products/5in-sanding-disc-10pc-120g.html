5" SANDING DISC 10PC 120G

These sanding discs feature a resin-bonded aluminum oxide grit, making them ideal for both wood and metal applications. They are also effective for sanding paint, fillers, primers, and more. Designed for use with sanders that have 8-hole pads, these 5" discs offer versatility and convenience. The hook-and-loop fastening system allows for quick and easy grit changes, enhancing workflow efficiency. Each package contains 10 sanding discs, perfect for a variety of sanding tasks.

Features

  • Resin-Bonded Aluminum Oxide Grit: Provides durability and efficient sanding for both wood and metal surfaces.
  • Wide Range of Usages: Suitable for sanding wood, metal, paint, fillers, primers, and other materials.
  • Quick Change Hook and Loop System: Allows for easy and fast grit changes, improving work efficiency.
  • 8-Hole Design: Specifically designed for use with 8-hole sanders, ensuring optimal dust extraction.

Specifications

  • Disc Size: 5".
  • Number of Holes: 8.
  • Grit: 120.
  • Package Quantity: 10 discs.

Includes

  • 10 sanding discs with 120 grit and hook-and-loop fastening.
