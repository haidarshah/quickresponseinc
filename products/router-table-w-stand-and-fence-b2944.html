ROUTER TABLE WITH FENCE AND STAND

This router table combo is a comprehensive solution for both professional workshops and home DIY enthusiasts. It features a robust M.D.F. table and fence equipped with a dust port, a universal table insert, and a durable steel stand with non-marring rubber feet to ensure stability and protect your work surface.

The router table measures 32 inches in length and 24 inches in width, made from 1-3/8 inch thick M.D.F. It includes a 32-inch long aluminum miter track and an 11-3/4 inch aluminum T-track, allowing for precise routing tasks. The fence, crafted from 1-inch thick M.D.F, is 18 inches long and 6 inches high, featuring a fluorescent plastic guard and an 18-inch aluminum miter track for enhanced safety and accuracy. The package includes a steel stand that is 34 inches tall, complete with four non-marring rubber feet for added stability.
