24" ALUMINUM T TRACK WITH 2 HOLD DOWNS

The 24" Aluminum T-Track Set With 2 Hold-Downs is a versatile tool designed for creating custom jigs, positioners, and hold-downs, saving you valuable set-up time in your workshop. This set includes a 24" aluminum T-track and two adjustable hold-downs that securely position and clamp workpieces. Each hold-down comes with a knob and a 1/4"-20 sliding bolt, and is adjustable to work with materials up to 2 1/2" thick, providing flexibility for a variety of woodworking projects.
