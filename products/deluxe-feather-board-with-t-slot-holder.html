DELUXE FEATHER BOARD WITH T SLOT HOLDER

The B3260 Retrofit Featherboard Kit with T-slot Holder is a versatile and essential safety tool for improving precision and control during woodworking tasks. Designed for use with router tables, table saws, and band saws, this high-quality featherboard kit ensures that your workpiece stays firmly in place while reducing the risk of kickback. With two T-slot locking nuts and knobs, the featherboard quickly attaches to your working surface and stays securely locked when tightened. This kit is an excellent addition to any workshop, enhancing safety and accuracy.

Key Features

  • Quick and Secure Attachment: Features two T-slot locking nuts with knobs for fast and reliable installation on working surfaces.
  • Enhanced Safety: Holds workpieces securely in place, reducing the risk of movement and kickback during operation.
  • Improved Precision: Ensures consistent and accurate cuts by keeping the material firmly aligned against the fence or table.
  • Versatile Applications: Compatible with router tables, table saws, and band saws for a wide range of woodworking projects.
  • Durable Construction: Built to withstand regular workshop use, ensuring long-lasting performance.

Specifications

  • Locking Mechanism: Two T-slot locking nuts with knobs
  • Compatible Tools: Router tables, table saws, and band saws
  • Screw Size: 1/4" x 2-1/4"
  • Knob Hole: 1/4" through hole
  • Material: High-quality components for durability and reliability

Comes With

  • Featherboard
  • Knob (1/4" through hole)
  • Washer (1/4")
  • Screw (1/4" x 2-1/4")
  • T-Slot Holder
