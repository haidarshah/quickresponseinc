LIVE CENTRE MT2 FOR WOOD LATHE

The B1320 Live Centre MT2 is designed for wood lathes, providing smooth and reliable rotation during turning operations. Featuring an MT2 taper, this live centre ensures a secure fit in the lathe tailstock for stability and precision.

Its cup centre design offers excellent grip and support for wooden workpieces, while the ball bearing mechanism reduces friction and heat buildup, leading to smoother operation and longer tool life.

Features:

  • MT2 taper – Ensures a secure and precise fit in wood lathes.
  • Cup centre design – Provides better workpiece stability and support.
  • Ball bearing construction – Reduces friction for smooth and efficient turning.
  • Durable and long-lasting – Built for consistent performance in woodworking applications.

Specifications:

  • Type: Live Centre
  • Taper Size: MT2
  • Centre Design: Cup Centre
  • Bearing Type: Ball Bearing

Comes With:

  • One MT2 Live Centre for Wood Lathes.
