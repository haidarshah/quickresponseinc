ROUTER BIT DEPTH GAUGE

The B1309 Router Bit Depth Gauge is an essential tool for woodworkers looking to achieve precise cutting depths without the guesswork. Designed for accuracy and ease of use, this gauge allows you to set and check router bit depths quickly and efficiently. Clearly marked in 16ths from 1/8" to 1", it enables you to achieve 15 precise depth settings without wasting time or material on trial and error. Whether you're routing a V-groove or working on intricate projects, this tool ensures consistent, professional results.

Features:

  • Accurately measures router bit depth for precise cuts
  • Clearly marked in 16ths from 1/8" to 1" for easy reading
  • Allows for 15 precise depth settings without trial and error
  • Saves time and prevents material waste
  • Compact and easy to use for any woodworking project

Specifications:

  • Measurement range: 1/8" to 1"
  • Markings: 16ths of an inch
  • Depth settings: 15 adjustable increments
  • Material: Durable and lightweight construction
