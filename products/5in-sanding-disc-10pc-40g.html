5" SANDING DISC 10PC 40G

These 5" sanding discs are designed with a resin-bonded aluminum oxide grit, making them suitable for a variety of applications, including sanding wood, metal, paint, fillers, primers, and more. The discs are ideal for both heavy-duty sanding and finer finishing tasks. The quick-change, hook and loop fastening system ensures easy switching between different grit levels, making your sanding projects more efficient. Each disc is crafted to fit sanders with 8-hole pads, allowing for optimal dust extraction and smoother sanding results.

Features

  • Versatile Use: Suitable for sanding wood, metal, paint, fillers, primers, and more.
  • Resin Bonded Aluminum Oxide Grit: Provides durability and ensures effective sanding performance.
  • Hook and Loop Fastening System: Allows quick and easy changes between discs, enhancing workflow.
  • 8-Hole Design: Compatible with 8-hole sanders for efficient dust removal.

Specifications

  • Disc Size: 5 inches
  • Number of Holes: 8
  • Grit: 40
  • Quantity: 10 discs per package
