TURNERS MESH SANDING PACK

Turners Mesh Abrasive Rolls are designed for efficient sanding projects on your lathe. Each abrasive strip features tiny holes that allow wood particles to pass through, preventing the abrasive surface from clogging. This makes the strips ideal for sanding rounded shapes such as spindles, vases, and bowls. The flexible material easily conforms to various shapes, ensuring a smooth finish.

Features:

  • Mesh construction allows dust particles to pass through, preventing clogging.
  • Extends the life of the abrasive mesh.
  • Flexible mesh conforms to rounded shapes, perfect for spindles, vases, and bowls.
  • Ideal for use on lathes.

Specifications:

  • Length per roll: 20 feet
  • Width per roll: 1 inch
  • Grits included: 180, 240, 320, 400, 600

Comes With:

  • 1 roll of 1" x 20' / 180 Grit
  • 1 roll of 1" x 20' / 240 Grit
  • 1 roll of 1" x 20' / 320 Grit
  • 1 roll of 1" x 20' / 400 Grit
  • 1 roll of 1" x 20' / 600 Grit
