CUSTOMIZABLE MOBILE BASE

The Customizable Mobile Base is an essential addition to any workshop, allowing you to create mobile bases tailored to fit any machine weighing up to 500 lbs. Designed for versatility and ease of use, this kit can be paired with plywood ranging from ½" to 1" thick. The mobile base is equipped with four rugged wheels, making it simple to maneuver your machinery when not in use. This solution optimizes workspace efficiency by enabling easy movement and storage of heavy equipment.

Features

1. Custom Fit for Any Machine: Adaptable to various machine sizes, ensuring a perfect fit for your specific equipment.

2. Low Profile Design: Adds only 1" to the overall height, maintaining a low center of gravity for stability.

3. Rugged Wheels: Four durable wheels provide smooth and reliable movement across the workshop floor.

4. High Load Capacity: Supports machines weighing up to 500 lbs, suitable for most workshop equipment.

5. Plywood Compatibility: Accommodates plywood thickness from ½" to 1", offering flexibility in base construction.

Specifications

  • Load Capacity: 500 lbs
  • Height Addition: 1"
  • Wheel Count: 4 rugged wheels
  • Plywood Thickness Compatibility: ½" to 1"
  • Material: Durable construction suitable for heavy machinery

Comes With

  • 1 x Customizable Mobile Base Kit
  • 4 x Rugged Wheels
  • Pre-drilled corner wheel pieces
