12" X 18" VARI SPEED WOOD LATHE



CX813

12" x 18" Mini Wood Lathe - Craftex CX-Series

The CX813 mini wood lathe is equipped with a 3/4HP motor and turns work-piece up to 16-1/2" in length. It comes with variable speed, ranging from 650 to 3800 RPM and features spindle digital readout which allows you to turn stock at the required speed. The On/Off switch on the lathe features a removable key which prevents from unauthorized operation.

Comes with a spur center, live center, 3-1/8" faceplate, knockout bar, a user manual and a 3 year warranty.

The CX813 can be extended another 22" by installing the Optional CX813EXT Bed Extension.

SPECIFICATIONS

Speed: 1700 RPM
Swing Over Bed: 12"
Swing Over Tool Rest Base: 9-1/2"
Distance Between Centers: 16-1/2"
Spindle TPI: 1" x 8 TPI
Spindle Taper: MT2
Tailstock Taper: MT2
Tailstock Center Type: Live

Spindle Speed Ranges: 650 - 3800 RPM
Faceplate Size: 3-1/8"
Bed Width: 7-1/4"
Bed Construction: Cast Iron
Headstock Construction: Cast Iron
Tailstock Construction: Cast Iron
Dimensions: 39" L x 12" W x 17" W
Approx. Weight: 84 lbs.
