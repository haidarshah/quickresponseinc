ON OFF FOOT SWITCH FOR MACHINERY

The B2855 On/Off Foot Switch is a practical and versatile solution for hands-free operation of workshop machinery and household equipment. Designed for efficiency and safety, this foot switch allows you to power tools like scroll saws, table saws, routers, and drill presses with ease. It can also be used for home applications, such as sewing machines. The switch engages power only when stepped on and automatically turns off when released, ensuring maximum control. Built to high standards, it is CSA-approved and manufactured in an ISO 9001-certified factory, offering reliability and peace of mind.

Features

  • Hands-Free Operation: Ideal for workshop tools and household equipment, improving efficiency and safety.
  • Momentary Action: Powers ON only when pressed and switches OFF upon release, offering precise control.
  • Versatile Applications: Suitable for scroll saws, table saws, routers, drill presses, sewing machines, and more.
  • High Safety Standards: CSA-approved for safety and reliability.
  • Durable Construction: Made in an ISO 9001-certified factory for consistent quality and performance.

Specifications

  • Power Rating: 115 volts, 15 amps.
  • Switch Type: Momentary ON/OFF foot-activated switch.
  • Certifications: CSA-approved; manufactured in an ISO 9001-certified facility.
  • Applications: Suitable for a wide range of machinery and equipment in workshops and homes.
