ON OFF FOOT SWITCH FOR MACHINERY

The B2855 On/Off Foot Switch is a practical and versatile solution for hands-free operation of workshop machinery and household equipment. Designed for efficiency and safety, this foot switch allows you to power tools like scroll saws, table saws, routers, and drill presses with ease. It can also be used for home applications, such as sewing machines. The switch engages power only when stepped on and automatically turns off when released, ensuring maximum control. Built to high standards, it is CSA-approved and manufactured in an ISO 9001-certified factory, offering reliability and peace of mind.
