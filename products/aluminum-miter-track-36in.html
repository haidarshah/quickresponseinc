36" ALUMINUM MITER T TRACK

The B3263 Aluminum Miter Track is a versatile and durable 36-inch miter track designed to fit standard 3/4" x 3/8" miter gauges, with or without a T-slot. It offers smooth sliding for precise adjustments, making it ideal for various woodworking applications. This track is compatible with different tools, including table saws, drill press tables, band saw tables, and table saw extensions, enhancing their functionality and accuracy. The B3263 miter track is crafted from high-quality aluminum, ensuring long-lasting performance and reliability. It also comes with 5 pre-drilled holes for easy and secure mounting, saving you installation time.

Features

  • Universal Compatibility: Works with standard 3/4" x 3/8" miter gauges, suitable for both T-slot and non-T-slot configurations.
  • Versatile Usage: Can be used with table saws, drill press tables, band saw tables, and table saw extensions.
  • Smooth Sliding: Provides a smooth and precise sliding action for accurate adjustments.
  • High-Quality Aluminum Construction: Ensures durability, strength, and longevity.
  • Pre-Drilled Holes: Features 5 pre-drilled holes for quick and easy mounting.
  • Corrosion-Resistant: Made of aluminum to prevent rust and ensure long-term usage.

Specifications

  • Length: 36 inches
  • Width: Compatible with 3/4" miter gauges
  • Height: Compatible with 3/8" miter gauges
  • Material: Aluminum
  • Mounting Holes: 5 pre-drilled holes for secure attachment
  • T-Slot Compatibility: Works with and without T-slots

Comes With

  • 1 x B3263 Aluminum Miter Track (36")
  • Pre-drilled mounting holes for quick and easy installation
