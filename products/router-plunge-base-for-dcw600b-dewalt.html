ROUTER PLUNGE BASE FOR DCW600B DEWALT

The DNP612 heavy-duty Plunge Base for Compact Router features a clear, Lexan base for enhanced base durability and rubber overmolded handles and low center of gravity design for comfort, balance, and control. Features: Adjustable, tool-free steel motor cam lock makes depth adjustment and base changes quick and solid-locking.
