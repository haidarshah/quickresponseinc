ROUTER PLUNGE BASE FOR DCW600B DEWALT

The DNP612 heavy-duty Plunge Base for Compact Router features a clear, Lexan base for enhanced base durability and rubber overmolded handles and low center of gravity design for comfort, balance, and control.

Adjustable, tool-free steel motor cam lock makes depth adjustment and base changes quick and solid-locking.

Easy to reach plunge lever and switch for superior control while routing.
