Our Story

Since 2005, Quick Response Inc. has specialized in sourcing and supplying professional-grade woodworking machinery to shops across Ontario and beyond. We partner with leading manufacturers to offer table saws, planers, sanders, and CNC equipment that meet the highest standards of performance and reliability.

Our Mission

To provide woodworkers with the right tools—backed by transparent pricing and rapid delivery—so they can focus on craftsmanship rather than equipment headaches.

Core Values

  • Integrity: Honest advice and straightforward quotes, always.
  • Quality: Only machinery we trust in our own workshop.
  • Efficiency: Fast order processing and reliable shipping across Canada.
  • Support: Expert pre- and post-sale guidance to keep your machines running.

Key Milestones

  • 2005: Launched as a local distributor of CSA-certified saws and planers.
  • 2012: Expanded product line to include CNC routers and digital readouts.
  • 2018: Established nationwide shipping partnerships for faster delivery.
  • 2023: Added eco-efficient machinery options to our catalogue.
